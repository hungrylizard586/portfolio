Alliance to Mobilize Our Resistance
(Deportation Defense in RI)

Alliance to Mobilize Our Resistance (AMOR) is a nonprofit organization in Rhode Island that organizes to interrupt state violence and supports people impacted by the inhumane immigration system. They do this through community organizing, advocacy and by creating safe, healing, accessible, and empowering community spaces. AMOR fights for a world where humane immigration practices are guaranteed and where crossing borders is a natural and safe part of life, especially for our Black and Indigenous relatives. A world with equitable access to basic resources and services for migrants, including mental and physical health, housing, food, language and education, and where migrants are guaranteed due process under the law. Read more about their work here.

Providence Youth Student Movement
(Refugee Support and Defense in RI)
Providence Youth Student Movement (PrYSM) is supporting over 70 Southeast Asian refugees with deportation orders. They provide organizing, legal, and community resources for our members and their families to fight against the US deportation machine. With your support, PrYSM can continue to:
  • provide rapid response for community members detained and facing immediate deportation
  • provide mutual aid for legal fees and services
  • support impacted members and their families during ICE check ins and court hearings
  • provide case management for current and new members with orders of deportation
Read more about their work here.
Middle Eastern Children's Alliance
(Palestinian Community Project Support)

The Middle East Children's Alliance (MECA) works to protect the rights and improve the lives of children in the Middle East through aid, empowerment and education. In the Middle East, MECA provides humanitarian aid, partners with community organizations to run projects for children, and supports income-generation projects. In the US and internationally, MECA raises awareness about the lives of children in the region and encourages meaningful action. Read more about their work here.
